Main Spices

Mace

Mace is the lacy, red-orange outer covering of the nutmeg seed.

Nutmeg

Nutmeg is a warm, aromatic spice derived from the seed of the nutmeg tree.

Cardamon

Cardamom is a fragrant spice with a complex flavor profile that is both sweet and spicy, with hints of citrus and mint.

Cloves

Cloves are aromatic flower buds with a warm, sweet, and slightly bitter flavor, often described as intense and spicy.

Dried Ginger

Dried ginger is a potent spice made from fresh ginger root that has been dried and ground into a powder.

Turmeric

Turmeric is a vibrant yellow-orange spice known for its earthy, slightly bitter flavor and warm aroma.

Black Pepper

Black pepper is a bold, pungent spice known for its sharp, spicy flavor.

Garcinia

Garcinia is a tropical fruit known for its tangy, slightly sour flavor.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon is a warm, aromatic spice derived from the inner bark of trees, known for its sweet and slightly spicy flavor that enhances both sweet and savory dishes
